How To Choose The Best Betting Site

1The Welcome Offer

Pick a welcome bonus that is right for you. Some will be better suited to those looking to bet big, while others are better for more casual punters. Betting is meant to be fun, so don’t stretch yourself, and bet responsibly.

2Ongoing Offers

Each bookmaker will have offers for existing members, plus loyalty schemes which can be accessed on either the desktop version of their website, or their mobile apps. These vary with different bookmakers so look around for the best value promotional offers.

3Live Streaming

Some betting sites allow you to live stream a sporting event. To do so, you might need to bet on the event, or at least have credit in your balance. Plenty of bookies allow you to stream various events across the sporting calendar.

4Betting Markets

If you’re going to bet on a range of sports and leagues, research which bookmakers cover the markets you need. All betting websites will have markets for the most popular sports and competitions. But not all sites cover obscure leagues or events.

5Odds

Everyone wants to win as much as possible. It is challenging to know who has the best odds because all bookmakers boast that they have them! Most punters often have several accounts and place their bet with whoever has the best odds for a particular event.

6Interface

This, again, comes down to personal preference. Each bookmaker has a slightly different interface. As long as it’s not slow, and is easy to use, then it should be fine. Some are more modern than others.

7Mobile Experience

Mobile apps are the go-to way to access many bookmakers nowadays, as punters can access all the features they need whilst on-the-go, which is hugely popular. Each mobile app is different though, with some being more easy to navigate around than others.

8Industry Reputation

As with every business, bookmakers thrive on reputation. Reputation isn’t the be-all and end-all, but it can give you a good idea on which bookies are worth considering. There’s a reason why the industry leaders maintain their regular client base.

9Payment Methods & Banking Options

The safety and security of your money are extremely important. We recommend you choose a bookmaker who offers a variety of payment options, including debit card and e-wallet facilities. In addition, ensure your choice offers the latest, up-to-date security for your selected payment methods.

10Sign-Up Process

With most online bookmakers, the sign-up process should only take a few minutes. Note that most bookmakers now employ a Know Your Customer scheme. They may request that you provide additional documents to prove your identity.

This remains one of the most popular payment methods for punters across the UK, with deposits being instant into the customer’s betting account with their chosen bookmaker. Some betting accounts process withdrawals within two hours of the withdrawal request being made but this is dependent on the bank account. You can also use Apple Pay online with your chosen bookmaker, with deposits and withdrawals being quick to and from a customer’s bank account, although some withdrawals can take up to 48 hours at times.

Deposits are instant from PayPal to most bookmaking accounts although some withdrawals to and from a PayPal account can take slightly longer depending on the customer’s bank.
